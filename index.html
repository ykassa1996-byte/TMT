Instructions

Part A: You will see 25 circles numbered 1 through 25. Use your mouse or finger to connect the numbers in ascending order (1-2-3...). If you click a wrong circle, it will flash red.

Part B: You will see circles containing numbers (1-13) and letters (A-L). You must connect them in an alternating ascending pattern: 1-A-2-B-3-C, and so on. A line will draw between circles as you progress.

Work as quickly and accurately as possible. Your time and errors will be recorded for both parts.
